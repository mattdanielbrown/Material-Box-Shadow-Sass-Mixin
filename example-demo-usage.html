material-shadow.scss

A mixin based on Material Design box shadows with 5 presets and a strength setting.

sample usage:

@include material-shadow($z-depth: 3); @include material-shadow(3);
$z-depth: 1
$z-depth: 2
$z-depth: 3
$z-depth: 4
$z-depth: 5

Increase strength for darker backgrounds

sample usage:

@include material-shadow($z-depth: 3, $strenght: 2);

$z-depth: 1, $strength: 2
$z-depth: 2, $strength: 2
$z-depth: 3, $strength: 2
$z-depth: 4, $strength: 2
$z-depth: 5, $strength: 2
